How Verdi & Co. handles personal information on this website.

Privacy

An informational website.

Last updated: 21 May 2026

Controller / Operator identity

This website, www.theverdico.com, is operated under the business name Verdi & Co. For privacy-related enquiries, please contact: info@theverdico.com.

Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Verdi & Co. approaches personal information in connection with this website. This website is informational. It does not provide user accounts, online payments, gated downloads, newsletter registration or a website contact form.

Information that may be processed

Verdi & Co. does not intentionally collect personal data through forms, account registration, payment systems or newsletter sign-ups.

Limited technical information — such as IP addresses, browser type and access timestamps — may be processed automatically by the server or hosting infrastructure that delivers this website. This processing is a standard technical feature of internet communication, is not controlled directly by Verdi & Co., and is not used to build user profiles or target individuals.

The website may also load technical assets from third-party content delivery or infrastructure providers. Those providers may process limited technical data, such as IP address, browser information, request metadata and server logs, for delivery, security, diagnostics and abuse-prevention purposes.

If you contact Verdi & Co. voluntarily by email or other means, the information you provide — including your name, contact details and the content of your message — may be processed in order to respond to your enquiry and manage the relevant professional communication.

Legal basis for processing

Where personal data is processed, the applicable legal basis may include: our legitimate interests in operating, maintaining and securing this website; the need to take steps prior to entering into, or to perform, a contract or professional engagement; or compliance with a legal obligation. Where you contact Verdi & Co. voluntarily, the communication you initiate may form the basis of processing.

International transfers

Where personal data is transferred internationally and applicable data protection law requires safeguards, Verdi & Co. will rely on an appropriate lawful transfer mechanism, such as an adequacy decision, standard contractual clauses, UK international data transfer terms, or another mechanism recognised by applicable law.

Storage and retention

Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law.

Transaction and professional correspondence may be retained for the duration of the relevant matter and for any applicable limitation, regulatory, tax, accounting or dispute period thereafter.

Your rights

Depending on applicable data protection law, you may have rights including: access to personal data held about you; rectification of inaccurate data; erasure in certain circumstances; restriction of processing; data portability; and the right to object to certain processing. Where processing is based on consent, you have the right to withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact: info@theverdico.com.

No client relationship

No confidential or legally privileged relationship is created merely by visiting this website or sending an unsolicited message.

Contact

For any privacy-related enquiry or to exercise your rights, please contact: info@theverdico.com.
