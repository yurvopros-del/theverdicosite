About cookies and similar technologies

Cookies and similar technologies are small files or data entries stored in your browser or on your device. They can help websites function, remember preferences and — in some implementations — track behaviour across sessions or sites.

Our approach: essential only

This website is operated on an essential-only basis. Verdi & Co. does not intentionally use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, marketing pixels, behavioural tracking technologies or social-media tracking pixels.

No tracking tools embedded

This website does not embed any of the following: Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, Meta Pixel, LinkedIn Insight Tag, Hotjar, Microsoft Clarity, Yandex Metrica, advertising networks, retargeting pixels, newsletter tracking or marketing scripts.

Strictly necessary technologies

Strictly necessary technologies may be used only to deliver the pages of this website, maintain security and support HTTPS connections, and prevent abuse. If a cookie-preference notice is displayed on this website, a preference may be stored to avoid showing the notice repeatedly. No personal data is collected through any strictly necessary mechanism described above.

Browser controls

You can manage, restrict or delete cookies and similar storage using your browser’s settings. Guidance is available from your browser provider. Because this website does not use non-essential cookies, blocking cookies will not materially affect your experience on this site.
