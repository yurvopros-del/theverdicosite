Principles

Verdi & Co.'s Business Principles

In a nutshell

Cooperation needs rhythm before activity.

Dear friends and colleagues,

At Verdi & Co., we believe in adhering to essential principles that serve as the foundation for meaningful and successful cooperation. We expect these principles to be upheld by individuals and company representatives with whom we engage professionally. These principles are straightforward yet crucial in achieving outstanding results:

  1. 1
    Timelines

    We strive to adhere to agreed-upon timelines whenever feasible.

  2. 2
    Clarity, consistency, and predictability

    Communication should be clear, consistent, and predictable to foster effective collaboration.

  3. 3
    Advance notice

    In the event of unforeseen circumstances, providing advance notice is essential.

  4. 4
    Responsibility

    We value accountability for one's words and actions.

  5. 5
    Accuracy

    Demonstrating accuracy is a fundamental indicator of competence.

If these principles align with your vision, we are confident that we can engage in fruitful cooperation. However, if these principles do not resonate, we believe that our shared potential as economic agents would be limited, making it impractical in the realm of business.

Of course, we can always maintain our friendship regardless of business considerations.

Sincerely

Independent contractors. International reach. Unparalleled motivation, reliability and professionalism across real estate, energy, M&A and law.
